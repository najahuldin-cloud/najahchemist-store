Common questions

About the manufacturing process

What is the difference between ready-made and custom products?
Ready-made products are available immediately. Custom formulations take 7–21 days. Ready-made products are formulas already developed and tested by Najah Chemist — you choose from our existing catalogue and we produce your order with no development time. Custom formulations are developed to your specific requirements, which takes longer but gives you a unique product. For most beginners, ready-made is the fastest and safest starting point. See which option is right for your stage →
Can Najah Chemist supply the containers and bottles?
Yes. Containers are available as an optional add-on from Najah Chemist. We supply 2oz bottles for liquid products and 4oz jars for thick products like body butter and scrubs. You can purchase containers from us or source your own from any supplier — the formula we produce goes inside whatever container you choose. Learn more about our manufacturing process →
Do I need a label or brand name before placing my first order?
No. You can place your order before your label is finalised. Most clients receive their bulk product first, then apply their label before selling. We recommend having your label ready before your order arrives so you can start selling immediately — but it is not required before production begins. See the full step-by-step guide →
How fast can I start selling after ordering?
With ready-made products, you can start selling within 2–5 days of placing your order. Ready-made products are manufactured in 2–3 business days. Once received, label your containers and start selling on Instagram or WhatsApp immediately — no website required. Custom formulations take 7–21 days, meaning your first sale comes 2–4 weeks from order date depending on how quickly you have containers and labels ready. See what it costs to get started →
What happens after I place my order at Najah Chemist?
After payment, we begin manufacturing your product and confirm your timeline. Here is the exact sequence: 1) You place your order and complete payment. 2) We confirm receipt and provide your ready date — typically 2–3 days for ready-made, 7–21 days for custom. 3) Your order is manufactured in bulk. 4) We arrange delivery via Knutsford Express, Zipmail, or Kingston/St. Andrew hand delivery. 5) You receive your bulk product, fill your containers, apply your label, and start selling. You can start from as little as J$3,550 for a single product. The full range is J$3,550 to J$100,000 depending on which products you choose and what quantities you start with.
