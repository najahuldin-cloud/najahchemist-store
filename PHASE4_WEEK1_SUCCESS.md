# PHASE4_WEEK1_SUCCESS.md — Week 1 Success Plan

Purpose: define what "Phase 4 is working" means in the first 7 days of real use, with
measurable thresholds. Jarvis is a decision system — success = better/faster decisions
and influenced revenue, not dashboard views.

| Metric | Definition | Week-1 target | Stretch | Red flag |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Daily usage** | Days Najah opens `/jarvis` | ≥ 5 of 7 | 7 of 7 | ≤ 2 |
| **Recommendations followed** | Founder Focus / Top Actions acted on | ≥ 1 action/day | ≥ 3/day | 0 most days |
| **Revenue influenced** | J$ from leads contacted via Jarvis recommendations (attributed loosely) | ≥ J$150,000 booked/quoted | ≥ J$300,000 | J$0 |
| **Opportunities discovered** | Leads Najah would NOT have actioned without Jarvis | ≥ 5 | ≥ 15 | 0 |
| **Recommendation accuracy** | Founder rating "Was Jarvis correct?" (1–10) avg | ≥ 7 | ≥ 8.5 | ≤ 5 |
| **Time saved** | Minutes/day vs manual lead triage (founder estimate) | ≥ 20 min/day | ≥ 45 min/day | net negative |
| **Founder satisfaction** | End-of-week 1–10 | ≥ 7 | ≥ 9 | ≤ 5 |
| **Data trust** | Honest Pipeline accepted as the number Najah quotes | yes | yes | reverts to "raw" |

## Leading indicators (check daily)
- Founder Focus action is specific and doable (a name + channel), not vague.
- Top Actions feel ranked right (highest-leverage first).
- Zero obvious test/duplicate leads surfaced in any card.

## Week-1 verdict
- **PASS:** ≥ 5 usage days, accuracy ≥ 7, ≥ J$150k influenced, satisfaction ≥ 7.
- **CONDITIONAL:** usage or accuracy slightly under target → tune copy/ordering in Phase 5, not architecture.
- **FAIL:** ≤ 2 usage days OR accuracy ≤ 5 OR J$0 influenced → stop, interview the founder, fix the top friction before any new build.

These thresholds are starting estimates for a single founder + ~473 honest leads; recalibrate after Week 1 with real data.
