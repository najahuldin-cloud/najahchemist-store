FAQ

Common questions, answered

What is the minimum order quantity at Najah Chemist? +

There is no strict minimum order — you can order as little as 1 litre or 24 bars of soap. However, bulk orders get better pricing. Most wholesale customers order by the litre, gallon, or 5-gallon size depending on their business needs.

Does Najah Chemist offer private label skincare? +

Yes. Najah Chemist offers full private label services — you can sell our products under your own brand name with your own labels and packaging. This is available for soaps, creams, serums, yoni care and more. Lead time is 7–14 business days for private label orders.

How long does it take to process and deliver an order? +

Standard wholesale orders are processed within 2–3 business days. Delivery is available island-wide via Knutsford Express (bearer fee J$500), Zipmail (approximately J$1,000), or Kingston & St. Andrew local delivery from J$1,000.

What ingredients does Najah Chemist use? +

Najah Chemist uses professional-grade actives including kojic acid, AHAs, glycolic acid, turmeric, vitamin C, hyaluronic acid and other efficacy-focused ingredients. We are not a natural or organic brand — we focus on results.

What payment methods does Najah Chemist accept? +

We accept bank transfer (NCB — JMD account 354-747-294, USD account 354-747-308), Lynk (@najahchemist), and card payments via Fygaro on najahchemistja.com. No cash on delivery — full payment required before processing.

Where is Najah Chemist located and do you ship island-wide? +

Najah Chemist is based in Kingston, Jamaica. We ship island-wide through Knutsford Express and Zipmail courier services, and offer local delivery within Kingston and St. Andrew.

What skincare products does Najah Chemist sell wholesale? +

Najah Chemist sells wholesale professional-grade skincare including: bar soaps (kojic, turmeric, vitamin C, charcoal), yoni care (foaming wash, scrub, oil, pearls), body creams, lightening creams, serums, body scrubs, and capsules. All products are available in bulk sizes.
