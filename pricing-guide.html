The Simple Pricing Formula

The Simple Pricing Formula

Retail Price = 2.5x – 4x your wholesale cost. Use 2.5x for competitive entry-level pricing, 3x for standard retail, and 4x+ for premium or niche brand positioning. Your ingredients justify your price — here's what that means for each product.

Strategy

4 Tips for Pricing Your Brand

1
Start at 3x your cost — gives you room to run promotions without cutting into profit.
2
Price by ingredient — products with named actives (Kojic Acid, Salicylic Acid, Hyaluronic Acid) command 20–40% more than unbranded equivalents.
3
Bundle for higher order value — pair a soap + serum + cream as a routine kit. Clients perceive value, you move more volume.
4
Never compete on price — Jamaica's skincare market rewards results and trust. If your product works, clients will pay premium.
