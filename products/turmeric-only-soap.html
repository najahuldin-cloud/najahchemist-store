About this product

What is the Najah Chemist Turmeric Only Soap?

The Najah Chemist Turmeric Only Soap is a cold-process bar soap formulated with a single powerful brightening active — Turmeric. Curcumin, the active compound in turmeric, inhibits tyrosinase and reduces melanin production with consistent daily use — gradually fading dark spots and evening out skin tone. Paired with saponified oils and Vitamin E, the soap cleanses gently while delivering brightening benefits with every wash.

Suitable for face and body use, the Turmeric Only Soap is a simple, effective, and customer-recognisable product. Each pack contains 10 × 4oz bars — ready for your label and immediate resale. The ingredient-forward name makes it easy to market and easy for customers to understand the benefit.

⚠ For external use only. Avoid contact with eyes. Do a patch test before first use. Keep out of reach of children.
Formulation

Key ingredients & benefits

Full INCI: Saponified Oils, Curcuma Longa Root Powder, Tocopherol, Fragrance, Preservatives

Turmeric
Curcuma Longa Root Powder

The sole brightening active. Curcumin inhibits melanin production, fades dark spots, and evens skin tone over time. Anti-inflammatory properties soothe the skin and reduce redness. A universally recognised natural brightening ingredient with strong customer appeal in Jamaica and the Caribbean.

Saponified Oils
Saponified Vegetable Oils

The soap base — a blend of plant-derived oils converted through saponification. Cleanses the skin gently while leaving a moisture film that prevents dryness. Cold-process soap retains more natural glycerin than commercial detergent bars, making it gentler on the skin.

Vitamin E
Tocopherol

Antioxidant that protects skin cells from oxidative damage. Nourishes and conditions skin during cleansing — supporting a healthy skin barrier and smooth texture. Also helps extend the shelf life of the natural turmeric extract in the formulation.

Fragrance
Fragrance

A clean, pleasant scent that makes daily use an enjoyable experience. Formulated to complement the natural earthiness of turmeric without overpowering it. Suitable for face and body use without irritation.

Usage instructions

How to use it

1
Wet skin with warm water.
2
Lather the soap in your hands or on a washcloth to build a rich lather.
3
Apply to face or body and gently massage in circular motions for 30–60 seconds.
4
Rinse thoroughly with water and pat dry with a clean towel.
5
Use daily. Follow with moisturiser. Apply SPF in the morning when using brightening products.
