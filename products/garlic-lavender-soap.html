About this product

What is the Najah Chemist Garlic & Lavender Soap?

The Najah Chemist Garlic & Lavender Soap is a cold process bar soap formulated to target acne, bacteria, and excess oil. Garlic extract is a proven natural antibacterial — it fights acne-causing bacteria, reduces breakouts, and helps control sebum production in oily skin types. Lavender essential oil complements the garlic with its anti-inflammatory and soothing properties, calming redness and irritation after cleansing.

Built on a base of Coconut Oil, Palm Oil, and Shea Butter, the soap delivers a creamy, conditioning lather that cleanses without stripping the skin barrier — important for sensitive and reactive skin types. Each pack contains 10 × 4oz bars, ready for your label and immediate resale.

⚠ For external use only. Avoid contact with eyes. Do a patch test before first use. Keep out of reach of children. Not recommended for use during pregnancy without medical advice.
Formulation

Key ingredients & benefits

Key ingredients: Garlic Extract, Lavender Essential Oil, Coconut Oil, Palm Oil, Shea Butter

Garlic Extract
Allium Sativum Bulb Extract

Natural antibacterial and antifungal agent. Fights acne-causing bacteria (P. acnes), helps clear existing breakouts, and reduces excess oil production in oily skin types. Rich in allicin — a potent antimicrobial compound with well-documented skin benefits.

Lavender Essential Oil
Lavandula Angustifolia Oil

Calms inflammation, soothes redness, and reduces skin irritation. Balances the potency of garlic extract for a gentler cleansing experience. Naturally antiseptic and antifungal — supports overall skin clarity and a clean, fresh scent.

Coconut Oil & Palm Oil
Cocos Nucifera Oil, Elaeis Guineensis Oil

The cleansing base of the soap. Coconut oil produces a rich, bubbly lather that deep-cleans pores. Palm oil adds hardness to the bar and contributes a conditioning quality that prevents the over-drying effect common in antibacterial soaps.

Shea Butter
Butyrospermum Parkii Butter

Adds emollient richness to the formula, leaving skin soft and moisturised after washing. Particularly beneficial for sensitive skin that may otherwise react to antibacterial actives. Helps maintain the skin barrier while the actives do their work.

Usage instructions

How to use it

1
Wet skin with warm water.
2
Lather the soap in your hands or on a washcloth to build a rich lather.
3
Apply to face or body and gently massage in circular motions for 30–60 seconds. Focus on problem areas.
4
Rinse thoroughly with cool water and pat dry with a clean towel.
5
Use once or twice daily. Follow with a lightweight moisturiser. Apply SPF in the morning.
