About this product

What is the Najah Chemist Glycolic Acid Soap?

The Najah Chemist Glycolic Acid Soap is an AHA exfoliating bar formulated with Glycolic Acid, Hibiscus, and Watermelon Extract. Glycolic Acid — the smallest AHA molecule — penetrates deep into the skin to dissolve dead cells, resurface texture, and fade dark spots with regular use.

Hibiscus adds a natural AHA boost and antioxidant support, while Watermelon Extract provides hydration and soothes the skin to counterbalance the exfoliating action. The result is a bar soap that cleans, brightens, and resurfaces in one step.

Manufactured in Jamaica by Najah Chemist, this product is available wholesale for private label brands, beauty shops, and skincare retailers.
