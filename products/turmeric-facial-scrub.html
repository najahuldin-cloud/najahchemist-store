About this product

What is the Najah Chemist Turmeric Facial Scrub?

The Najah Chemist Turmeric Facial Scrub combines physical exfoliation with brightening actives to reveal smoother, more even-toned skin. Sugar crystals gently slough away dead skin cells while Turmeric's natural curcuminoids and Kojic Acid work to inhibit melanin production and fade existing hyperpigmentation. Sunflower Oil and Vitamin E nourish the freshly exfoliated skin to prevent dryness after use.

Unlike a standard scrub that only cleans the surface, this formulation delivers a brightening treatment at the same time — making it more effective and more valuable as a product in a customer's skincare routine. Use 2–3 times per week for progressive brightening results alongside a daily cleanser and moisturiser.

⚠ For external use only. Avoid contact with eyes. Do a patch test before use. Do not use on broken or sunburned skin. Keep out of reach of children.
Formulation

Key ingredients & benefits

Full INCI: Sugar, Helianthus Annuus (Sunflower) Seed Oil, Cocamidopropyl Betaine, Stearic Acid, Cetyl Alcohol, Curcuma Longa Root Powder, Kojic Acid, Tocopherol, Fragrance, Preservatives

Turmeric
Curcuma Longa Root Powder

The star brightening active. Curcumin inhibits tyrosinase — the enzyme responsible for melanin production — reducing dark spots and hyperpigmentation over time. Anti-inflammatory properties soothe freshly exfoliated skin and reduce redness.

Kojic Acid
Kojic Acid

A powerful melanin-inhibiting ingredient derived from fungi fermentation. Works synergistically with turmeric to fade dark spots, even skin tone, and prevent new hyperpigmentation from forming. One of the most effective brightening actives in skincare formulation.

Coco Betaine
Cocamidopropyl Betaine

Creates a mild lather during scrubbing that lifts impurities without over-stripping the skin. Gentle enough for facial use — non-irritating surfactant that complements the physical exfoliation of the sugar without causing dryness or disrupting the skin barrier.

Vitamin E
Tocopherol

Antioxidant protection for freshly exfoliated skin. Nourishes and conditions the skin barrier immediately after exfoliation, reducing the risk of dryness and protecting against environmental damage. Promotes a healthy, smooth skin texture with consistent use.

Usage instructions

How to use it

1
Wet your face with warm water to open pores and soften the skin before exfoliating.
2
Apply a small amount to your face and gently massage in circular motions for 1–2 minutes.
3
Focus on dark spots and areas of uneven texture — do not apply excessive pressure.
4
Rinse thoroughly with warm water and pat dry with a clean towel.
5
Follow with moisturiser. Use 2–3 times per week. Always apply SPF in the morning when using brightening products.
⚠ Label warnings to include:
  • For external use only — avoid contact with eyes
  • Do a patch test before first use
  • Do not use on broken or sunburned skin
  • Use SPF during the day when using brightening products
  • Keep out of reach of children
