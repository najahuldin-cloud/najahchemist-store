About this product

What is the Boric Acid & Probiotics Cleanser?

The Najah Chemist Boric Acid & Probiotics Yoni Cleanser is a science-forward intimate wash designed to restore and maintain the vaginal pH in its optimal acidic range. Boric Acid is clinically recognised for its ability to support pH balance and inhibit the overgrowth of odour-causing bacteria and yeast. Paired with Lactobacillus Ferment — a probiotic ingredient that actively feeds and supports the beneficial bacteria in the intimate microbiome — this formula works from two directions: corrective and protective.

Hibiscus Extract provides antioxidant support and a natural floral scent while Aloe Vera soothes and calms. The fruit extracts (Strawberry, Blueberry, Orange) deliver antioxidants and light brightening properties. This is a premium intimate cleanser positioned for customers who want results beyond basic freshness.

⚠ External use only. Use on the vulva only — do not insert internally. Rinse thoroughly. Avoid contact with eyes. Discontinue use if irritation occurs. Not recommended during pregnancy without medical advice.

How to use

Usage instructions

1
Apply a small amount to wet hands or a soft cloth. A little goes a long way — the formula is concentrated.
2
Cleanse the external vulva area only using gentle circular motions. Do not insert product internally.
3
Rinse thoroughly with warm water until all product is removed. Pat dry with a clean towel.
4
Use once daily or as needed. Suitable for daily use. For best results, follow with Yoni Oil or VagiMist as part of a complete routine.
Storage: Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Keep out of reach of children. Best used within 12 months of opening.
