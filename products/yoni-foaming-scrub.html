About this product

What is the Yoni Foaming Scrub?

The Najah Chemist Yoni Foaming Scrub is a pH-balanced exfoliating scrub designed for the external intimate area. Sugar crystals provide gentle physical exfoliation while Coco Betaine creates a light lather that lifts away dead skin cells and impurities without stripping natural moisture. Lactic Acid gently brightens and evens skin tone over time, and Aloe Vera soothes the skin during and after exfoliation.

This product is formulated specifically for the sensitivity of intimate skin — gentle enough for the delicate external area but effective enough to deliver smooth, refreshed results with regular use 1–2 times per week. It's a natural complement to a daily yoni wash and completes a full intimate skin care routine.

⚠ For external use only. Do not use internally. Do a patch test before first use. Discontinue use if irritation occurs. Keep out of reach of children.

Sugar

Physical exfoliant that buffs away dead skin cells with gentle abrasion. Dissolves gradually during use for softening exfoliation that won't scratch or irritate sensitive intimate skin.

Sunflower Oil
Helianthus Annuus Seed Oil

Lightweight emollient rich in linoleic acid. Softens and moisturises the skin without leaving a greasy residue, supporting the skin's natural barrier during and after exfoliation.

Coco Betaine
Cocamidopropyl Betaine

Gentle surfactant derived from coconut oil that creates a mild lather while cleansing. Safe for sensitive intimate skin — non-irritating and non-drying. Provides the foaming action that lifts away dead skin and impurities.

Stearic Acid
Stearic Acid

Fatty acid that acts as an emulsifier and texture enhancer. Gives the scrub its smooth, creamy consistency and helps condition the skin surface during use.

Coco Mea
Cocamide MEA

Coconut-derived foam booster that thickens the formula and stabilises lather. Enhances the foaming action for a richer, more consistent cleansing experience on the skin.

Cetearyl Alcohol
Cetearyl Alcohol

A blend of fatty alcohols that thickens and stabilises the formula. Emollient and non-irritating — conditions skin and improves the spreadability and texture of the scrub.

Cetyl Alcohol
Cetyl Alcohol

Fatty alcohol that acts as an emollient and thickener. Contributes to the smooth, creamy texture and helps ingredients spread evenly across the skin during use.

Vitamin E
Tocopherol

Antioxidant that protects skin cells from oxidative damage. Nourishes and conditions skin during exfoliation and helps maintain the moisture barrier for a healthy, smooth skin texture.

Fragrance
Fragrance

Added to provide a pleasant scent. Used at cosmetic-grade concentrations appropriate for use on sensitive intimate skin.

Preservatives
Preservatives

Antimicrobial system that ensures product safety and extends shelf life. Prevents microbial contamination during storage and repeated use.

EDTA
Disodium EDTA

Chelating agent that binds metal ions in the formula. Improves the stability of other ingredients and enhances the effectiveness of the preservative system.

How to use it

1
Apply a small amount to the external intimate area.
2
Gently massage in circular motions for 30–60 seconds — do not scrub aggressively.
3
Rinse thoroughly with warm water.
4
Use 1–2 times per week — do not use daily.
5
For external use only. Follow with a gentle moisturiser or yoni oil if desired.
