About this product

What is the Najah Chemist Yoni Cleanser?

The Najah Chemist Yoni Cleanser is a pH-balanced feminine wash formulated specifically for external intimate cleansing. Unlike regular soap — which typically has a pH of 9–10 and disrupts the vaginal environment — this cleanser is formulated at an acidic pH that matches and preserves the natural intimate microbiome. Lactic Acid reinforces this pH balance while Aloe Vera soothes and Peppermint Oil provides a gentle refreshing sensation.

Intimate care is one of the fastest-growing personal care categories in Jamaica as women become more educated about the risks of using regular soap on sensitive intimate skin. Customers who switch to a dedicated intimate cleanser stay loyal to the product and brand — the personal nature of the category creates exceptional repeat purchase rates.

⚠ For external use only. Do not use internally. Avoid contact with eyes. Discontinue use if irritation occurs. If irritation persists, consult a healthcare provider. Keep out of reach of children.

Usage instructions

How to use it

1
Apply a small amount to clean hands — a little goes a long way with this concentrated formula.
2
Gently cleanse the external intimate area — do not use internally; the formula is for external use only.
3
Rinse thoroughly with warm water — ensure the product is completely rinsed from the skin.
4
Pat dry gently — avoid harsh rubbing of sensitive skin after cleansing.
5
Use daily during bathing — the formula is designed for safe daily use as part of regular hygiene routine.
