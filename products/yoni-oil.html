About this product

What is the Najah Chemist Yoni Oil?

The Najah Chemist Yoni Oil is a lightweight, absorbing intimate oil designed to moisturise and nourish the external intimate skin after cleansing. Sweet Almond Oil provides deep hydration without clogging pores, while Jojoba Oil mimics the skin's natural sebum to restore the skin barrier after washing. Rose Hip Oil delivers antioxidants and supports skin regeneration, and Tea Tree Oil provides gentle antimicrobial protection.

Used after a yoni cleanser or shower, the oil locks in moisture, reduces dryness and irritation, and leaves intimate skin feeling soft and comfortable. The formula is concentrated — a few drops is all that's needed — making each litre bottle highly profitable for retail. A natural complement to any feminine care line.

⚠ For external use only. Apply to the external vulva area. Do not insert internally. Avoid contact with eyes. Patch test before first use. Discontinue if irritation occurs.

How to use

Usage instructions

1
Cleanse first with a yoni wash or during your regular shower routine. Pat the intimate area dry with a clean towel.
2
Dispense 3–5 drops into the palm of your hand. The oil is concentrated — less is more for most skin types.
3
Apply gently to the external vulva area using soft circular motions until the oil is absorbed. Do not insert internally.
4
Use daily after cleansing as part of your intimate care routine. Can also be used to soothe occasional dryness or irritation throughout the day.
