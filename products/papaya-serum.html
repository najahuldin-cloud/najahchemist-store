About this product

What is the Najah Chemist Papaya Serum?

The Najah Chemist Papaya Serum is a concentrated brightening serum formulated with a high concentration of skin-lightening actives — Papaya Extract, Vitamin C, Kojic Acid, Alpha Arbutin, and Lemon Extract. Together, these ingredients target dark spots, hyperpigmentation, post-acne marks, and uneven skin tone.

Papaya enzymes (papain) naturally exfoliate the skin surface while the brightening actives work beneath to inhibit melanin production. The result is visibly clearer, more radiant skin with consistent use.

Manufactured in Jamaica by Najah Chemist, this serum is available wholesale for private label brands, spas, salons, and skincare retailers.

How to use

Usage instructions

1
Apply a few drops to a clean face and neck.
2
Gently pat into skin until absorbed — do not rub.
3
Use morning and evening for best results.
4
Follow with a moisturiser to lock in hydration.
5
Always apply SPF 30+ during the day to protect brightened skin from UV damage.

Warning: For external use only. Avoid contact with eyes. Do a patch test before use. Discontinue use if irritation occurs. Always use SPF when using this product. Keep out of reach of children.
