About this product

What is the Najah Chemist Face Moisturizer?

The Najah Chemist Face Moisturizer is a lightweight, fast-absorbing daily cream formulated for all skin types. Hyaluronic Acid draws moisture from the environment into the skin, providing deep hydration without heaviness. Niacinamide (Vitamin B3) regulates sebum for oily skin, reduces pore appearance, and delivers brightening benefits over time. Aloe Vera base soothes and calms while Shea Butter provides the emollient richness that dry skin needs. Allantoin and Panthenol support skin barrier repair.

This formula sits at the intersection of hydration and skin health — it's not just a moisturiser but a daily treatment. Suitable for all genders and all skin types, it's the most versatile product in your line and an essential anchor product for any skincare brand.

Hyaluronic Acid
Sodium Hyaluronate

Holds up to 1,000× its weight in water. Draws moisture from the air into the skin and holds it there throughout the day. Provides immediate plumping hydration and reduces the appearance of fine lines caused by dehydration.

Niacinamide
Niacinamide (Vitamin B3)

Multi-functional active: regulates sebum production for oily skin, minimises pore appearance, brightens uneven skin tone, and strengthens the skin barrier. One of the most well-researched skincare ingredients with benefits for all skin types.

Aloe Vera
Aloe Barbadensis Leaf Juice

Replaces water as the base, delivering skin-soothing, anti-inflammatory benefits throughout the formula. Calms redness, reduces post-breakout irritation, and provides lightweight hydration as the first layer of moisture.

Allantoin
Allantoin

A skin-conditioning agent that promotes cell regeneration, soothes irritation, and supports barrier repair. Works with Panthenol (Vitamin B5) to heal and protect the skin barrier — especially beneficial for sensitive or compromised skin.

How to use

Usage instructions

1
Cleanse your face with a gentle facial cleanser and pat dry with a clean towel.
2
Apply to slightly damp skin — Hyaluronic Acid works best when there is some moisture on the skin for it to bind to. Apply within 60 seconds of washing.
3
Use a small amount — approximately a pea-sized amount for the full face. Dot across forehead, cheeks, nose, and chin, then blend upward and outward.
4
Use morning and evening. In the morning, follow with SPF 30+ before sun exposure. In the evening, apply as the last step of your routine.
