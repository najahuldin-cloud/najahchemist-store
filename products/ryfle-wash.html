About this product

What is Ryfle Wash?

Ryfle Wash is a pH-balanced men's body wash specifically designed for intimate grooming. Tea Tree Oil provides natural antimicrobial action that targets the bacteria responsible for body and intimate odour, while Peppermint delivers a fresh, invigorating cooling sensation that signals cleanliness. Witch Hazel acts as a natural astringent — toning and tightening skin while reducing bacteria levels. Aloe Vera and Panthenol soothe the skin during and after cleansing.

The formula is gentle enough for the sensitive skin of intimate areas yet effective enough for full-body use. The pH-balanced formula avoids disrupting the skin's natural microbiome. Men who use a dedicated intimate wash become loyal customers — once they experience the difference in cleanliness and freshness, they do not return to regular soap.

Tea Tree Oil
Melaleuca Alternifolia Leaf Oil

Clinically proven antimicrobial that eliminates the bacteria responsible for body odour and intimate odour. More effective at odour control than fragrance alone — it addresses the source rather than masking it. A key active in men's grooming for a reason.

Witch Hazel
Hamamelis Virginiana Water

Natural astringent that tones skin and reduces bacteria levels on the skin surface. Also reduces excess oil that contributes to odour in skin folds. Anti-inflammatory properties reduce irritation in sensitive grooming areas.

Peppermint Oil
Mentha Piperita Oil

Provides the signature fresh, cooling sensation that men associate with effective cleansing. The menthol in peppermint has mild antimicrobial properties and creates a long-lasting freshness sensation on the skin after rinsing.

Aloe Vera
Aloe Barbadensis Leaf Juice

Soothes and calms sensitive skin during cleansing. Replaces water as the base to deliver skin-nourishing benefits throughout the formula. Reduces irritation in areas prone to friction and sensitivity.

How to use

Usage instructions

1
Apply during shower with warm water. Warm water opens pores and allows the active ingredients to penetrate better.
2
Lather in hands or on a washcloth before applying. Using a washcloth increases exfoliation and helps the product spread more economically.
3
Cleanse the body and intimate areas thoroughly. The formula is suitable for use across the full body.
4
Rinse thoroughly with warm then cool water. The cool water rinse closes pores and extends the fresh feeling.
5
Use daily as part of your shower routine. For active men or those in high-heat environments, can be used twice daily.
