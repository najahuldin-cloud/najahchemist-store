About this product

What is the Rose Facial Serum?

The Najah Chemist Rose Facial Serum is built on a Rosa Damascena Flower Water base — giving every application the soothing, anti-inflammatory, and mildly antibacterial benefits of pure rose water from the very first layer. Rose Hip Oil delivers natural Vitamin A (Retinol precursors), Vitamin C, and essential fatty acids that support skin regeneration, fade scars and hyperpigmentation, and improve overall skin texture. Ascorbic Acid (Vitamin C) boosts brightening while Hyaluronic Acid provides deep, multi-layer hydration.

This is a gentle yet effective serum suitable for all skin types — including sensitive and reactive skin. The rose base makes it particularly well-suited for inflamed, redness-prone, or post-acne skin. Niacinamide and Panthenol support barrier repair and add brightening benefits. The formula is positioned as a luxury daily serum with strong visual and olfactory appeal for retail packaging.

Rose Water
Rosa Damascena Flower Water

The entire formula is built on a Rose Water base — giving every application anti-inflammatory, mildly antibacterial, and skin-soothing properties from the first layer. Reduces redness, calms reactive skin, and provides a gentle natural scent without synthetic fragrance.

Rose Hip Oil
Rosa Canina Seed Oil

One of the most nutrient-rich plant oils. Contains natural Vitamin A (which converts to Retinol in skin), Vitamin C, Omega-3 and Omega-6. Supports cell regeneration, fades scars and hyperpigmentation, and improves skin texture and firmness over time.

Vitamin C
Ascorbic Acid

Potent antioxidant that brightens skin by inhibiting melanin production and neutralising free radicals from UV exposure. Works synergistically with the natural Vitamin C in Rose Hip Oil — the two sources amplify each other's brightening and antioxidant effects.

Hyaluronic Acid
Sodium Hyaluronate

Multi-weight Hyaluronic Acid provides hydration at both the skin surface and deeper layers. Immediately plumps the appearance of fine lines and dehydration lines, leaving skin visibly softer and more supple after the first application.

How to use

Usage instructions

1
Cleanse the face and apply to slightly damp skin for best Hyaluronic Acid absorption.
2
Dispense 3–4 drops and warm between fingertips. The Rose Hip Oil in the formula benefits from being warmed slightly before application.
3
Press gently into the skin across the face and neck. The pressing technique encourages absorption without rubbing and is particularly kind to sensitive skin.
4
Allow 1–2 minutes to absorb before applying moisturiser. At night, can be used as the final step — the Rose Hip Oil provides a lightweight overnight treatment.
5
Use morning and/or evening. Use SPF 30+ in the morning when using Vitamin C-based products — UV exposure reduces Vitamin C efficacy and reverses brightening progress.
