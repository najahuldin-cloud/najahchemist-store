About this product

What is the Najah Chemist Glycolic Acid AHA Toner?

The Najah Chemist Glycolic Acid AHA Toner is an exfoliating liquid toner formulated with Glycolic Acid, Rose Water, Hyaluronic Acid, and a blend of botanical extracts including Hibiscus, Lavender, Cucumber, and Rose. Together these ingredients resurface the skin while maintaining hydration, making it suitable for daily use.

Unlike most AHA toners that leave skin dry, this formula includes Hyaluronic Acid and Sodium Lactate to replenish moisture during exfoliation. Rose Water and botanical extracts calm and soothe, balancing the chemical activity of Glycolic Acid for a gentler but effective exfoliating experience.

Manufactured in Jamaica by Najah Chemist, this product is available wholesale for private label skincare brands and retailers.

How to use

Usage instructions

1
Apply to a cotton pad after cleansing.
2
Sweep gently across face and neck — do not rinse off.
3
Use at night only to start, until skin has adjusted.
4
Build up to daily use gradually over 2–4 weeks.
5
Always apply SPF 30+ the following morning to protect skin.

Caution: For external use only. Avoid contact with eyes. Do a patch test before use. Use SPF when using this product. Start with 2–3 times per week. Keep out of reach of children.
