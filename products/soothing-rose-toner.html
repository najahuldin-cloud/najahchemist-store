About this product

What is the Najah Chemist Soothing Rose Toner?

A lightweight toning step that prepares the skin for serums and moisturisers. Rose Water and Aloe Vera soothe and calm the skin after cleansing. Niacinamide brightens and minimises pores. Lactic Acid maintains a smooth skin texture. Witch Hazel acts as a gentle astringent to refine pores and tone the skin.

Full INCI: Water, Rosa Damascena Flower Water, Aloe Barbadensis Leaf Juice, Glycerine, Hamamelis Virginiana Water, Niacinamide, Lactic Acid, Tocopherol, Fragrance, Preservatives

Formulation

Key Ingredients

Rose Water
Rosa Damascena Flower Water
Naturally antibacterial and anti-inflammatory. Provides floral freshness while soothing skin. Hydrating and calming — reduces redness and prepares skin to absorb serums effectively.
Aloe Vera
Aloe Barbadensis Leaf Juice
Soothes and calms skin immediately after cleansing. Anti-inflammatory — reduces redness, dryness, and any tightness from cleansing. Provides hydration without heaviness.
Niacinamide
Niacinamide (Vitamin B3)
Brightens skin tone, reduces dark spots, and minimises the appearance of pores. Strengthens the skin barrier and reduces water loss. One of the most versatile and well-tolerated brightening actives available.
Witch Hazel
Hamamelis Virginiana Water
Natural astringent that tones and tightens pores after cleansing. Reduces excess oil and surface bacteria. Refines skin texture and leaves skin feeling clean and refreshed.

How to use

Usage Instructions

1
Apply to cotton pad and sweep across clean face and neck — or spray directly
2
Use morning and evening after cleansing
3
Allow to absorb for 30 seconds
4
Follow with serum and moisturiser
5
Always apply SPF in the morning
Precautions
  • For external use only
  • Avoid contact with eyes
  • Patch test before use
  • Keep out of reach of children
