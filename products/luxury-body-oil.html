About this product

What is the Najah Chemist Luxury Body Oil?

The Najah Chemist Luxury Body Oil is a lightweight, silky body oil formulated with Jojoba Oil and Sweet Almond Oil as the primary moisturising agents — both prized for their fast absorption and skin-conditioning properties. Cyclomethicone gives the formula its signature silky, non-greasy finish while Vitamin E protects and nourishes.

Available in multiple fragrance options, this body oil is ideal for brand owners who want a premium-feeling product at a competitive wholesale price. The lightweight texture appeals to Jamaican consumers who prefer a fast-absorbing, non-sticky moisturiser — especially in warm weather. High repeat purchase rate among customers who discover it.

⚠ External use only. Avoid contact with eyes — rinse immediately with water if contact occurs. Do not apply to broken, infected, or severely irritated skin. Keep out of reach of children. Store in a cool dry place away from direct sunlight and heat. Keep away from open flame — oil-based product.
Formulation

Key ingredients & benefits

Full INCI: Mineral Oil, Cyclomethicone, Simmondsia Chinensis (Jojoba) Seed Oil, Prunus Amygdalus Dulcis (Sweet Almond) Oil, Tocopherol, Fragrance

Jojoba Oil
Simmondsia Chinensis Seed Oil

Technically a liquid wax that closely mimics the skin's natural sebum. Absorbs rapidly without clogging pores, leaving skin soft and conditioned. Suitable for all skin types including sensitive and acne-prone skin.

Sweet Almond Oil
Prunus Amygdalus Dulcis Oil

Rich in oleic and linoleic fatty acids. Deeply nourishing and conditioning — softens dry, rough skin while maintaining a lightweight, non-greasy texture. Widely used in premium body oil formulations.

Cyclomethicone
Cyclomethicone

Silicone-derived emollient that gives the formula its signature silky, luxurious slip. Evaporates cleanly after application, leaving no greasy residue — the key ingredient behind the fast-absorbing, non-sticky finish.

Tocopherol (Vitamin E)
Tocopherol

Antioxidant that protects skin from environmental stressors and oxidative damage. Supports skin repair, improves moisture retention, and helps stabilise the oil formula for longer shelf life.

Usage instructions

How to use it

1
Apply after showering — best used on slightly damp skin for maximum moisture absorption.
2
Dispense a small amount — a little goes a long way. Start with 3–5 drops for arms or legs.
3
Massage in circular motions — work into the skin starting from legs and working upward.
4
Allow to absorb — the formula absorbs quickly — 60–90 seconds before dressing is sufficient.
5
Use daily — morning use locks in post-shower moisture; evening use supports overnight skin repair.
⚠ Label warnings to include:
  • External use only — avoid contact with eyes
  • Do not apply to broken or infected skin
  • Keep out of reach of children
  • Discontinue if irritation occurs
  • Store in a cool dry place away from direct sunlight and heat
  • Keep away from open flame — oil-based product
