About this product

What is the Najah Chemist VagiMist?

The Najah Chemist VagiMist is a pH-balanced intimate freshness spray designed for external use throughout the day. Witch Hazel tones and tightens while naturally reducing odour-causing bacteria. Rose Water provides a pleasant floral freshness while Aloe Vera soothes and calms sensitive intimate skin on contact. Lactic Acid maintains the intimate area's natural acidic environment.

Unlike a yoni cleanser used during bathing, the VagiMist is a portable, no-rinse spray — designed to be used between showers, after exercise, during long work days, or whenever freshness is needed. The spray format creates a distinct product in any feminine care line that customers carry in their bag and use daily.

⚠ For external use only. Do not spray internally. Avoid contact with eyes. Discontinue use if irritation occurs. Keep out of reach of children.

Usage instructions

How to use it

1
Shake well before use — natural ingredients may separate between uses.
2
Spray onto the external intimate area — hold 4–6 inches away and apply 2–3 sprays as needed.
3
Allow to dry naturally — or pat gently with clean tissue. No rinsing required.
4
Use throughout the day — reapply as needed for continuous freshness after exercise, work, or travel.
5
Do not spray internally — for external intimate area use only. Store upright in a cool dry location.
⚠ Label warnings to include:
  • For external use only — do not use internally
  • Avoid direct contact with eyes
