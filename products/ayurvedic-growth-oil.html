About this product

What is the Ayurvedic Hair Growth Oil?

The Najah Chemist Ayurvedic Hair Growth Oil draws on centuries of Indian hair care tradition. Amla (Indian Gooseberry) — one of the richest natural sources of Vitamin C — strengthens hair from the follicle and reduces premature greying. Hibiscus conditions the hair shaft, reduces shedding, and promotes thickness. Fenugreek strengthens roots and reduces hair fall with consistent use.

These Ayurvedic herbs are infused into a base of Coconut Oil, Castor Oil, and Olive Oil — a deeply nourishing combination that penetrates the scalp and hair shaft, strengthening from the root. Rosemary and Peppermint Essential Oils stimulate scalp circulation to support healthy hair growth.

This is a pre-wash scalp treatment applied before shampooing. Regular use over 4–8 weeks is when results become most visible — which means customers who see improvement are highly likely to continue purchasing. This creates long-term brand loyalty in the hair care category.

How to use

Usage instructions

1
Part the hair into sections and apply the oil directly to the scalp along each part. Focus on areas of concern such as the hairline, temples, or thinning spots.
2
Massage gently for 5–10 minutes using the fingertips in circular motions. The massage is essential — it stimulates blood circulation to the follicles, which is as important as the oil itself.
3
Distribute through the lengths of the hair for additional conditioning benefit. Wrap hair or cover with a shower cap.
4
Leave on for 30 minutes minimum — or overnight for an intensive treatment. The longer the oil is in contact with the scalp, the deeper it penetrates.
5
Shampoo out thoroughly with a gentle shampoo. May require 2 rounds of shampooing to fully remove the oil. Use 2–3 times per week for best results.
