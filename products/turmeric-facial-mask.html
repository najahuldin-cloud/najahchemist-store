About this product

What is the Najah Chemist Turmeric Facial Mask?

A deep-cleansing brightening clay mask for weekly use. Kaolin clay draws out impurities and excess oil from pores. Turmeric and Kojic Acid deliver brightening actives while the mask sits on the skin — combining deep cleansing with active treatment in a single step. Aloe Vera and Rose Water prevent the clay from over-drying the skin.

Full INCI: Kaolin, Curcuma Longa Root Powder, Kojic Acid, Aloe Barbadensis Leaf Juice, Glycerine, Tocopherol, Rosa Damascena Flower Water, Fragrance, Preservatives

Formulation

Key Ingredients

Kaolin Clay
Kaolin
Gentle white clay that draws out impurities, excess oil, and environmental pollutants from pores without over-stripping the skin. Softer and less drying than bentonite — suitable for all skin types including sensitive skin.
Turmeric
Curcuma Longa Root Powder
Delivers brightening curcuminoids to the skin while the mask is applied. Inhibits melanin production, reduces dark spots, and provides anti-inflammatory benefits that soothe freshly cleansed pores.
Kojic Acid
Kojic Acid
Brightening active that works alongside turmeric to fade hyperpigmentation. Extended contact time during the mask application allows for deeper penetration than a rinse-off cleanser — maximising brightening results per use.
Aloe Vera
Aloe Barbadensis Leaf Juice
Prevents the clay from over-drying and tightening the skin during the mask. Soothes and hydrates — skin feels comfortable and nourished after rinsing, not tight or stripped.

How to use

Usage Instructions

1
Apply even layer to clean dry face avoiding eye area
2
Leave on for 10–15 minutes until partially dry
3
Rinse thoroughly with warm water
4
Pat dry
5
Follow with moisturiser
6
Use 1–2 times per week
Precautions
  • For external use only
  • Avoid contact with eyes
  • Patch test before use
  • Keep out of reach of children
