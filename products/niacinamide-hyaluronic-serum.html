About this product

What is the Niacinamide & Hyaluronic Serum?

The Najah Chemist Niacinamide & Hyaluronic Serum is a dual-action treatment serum that addresses both skin texture and hydration simultaneously. 10% Niacinamide — the clinically proven concentration — minimises pore appearance, controls sebum, brightens uneven tone, and strengthens the skin barrier. Hyaluronic Acid at multiple molecular weights delivers hydration at different skin depths, providing immediate surface plumping and deeper long-term moisture binding. Zinc PCA regulates sebum production at the follicle level while Peptides support collagen synthesis and skin elasticity.

This is a universal serum that benefits all skin types — oily skin gets pore control and sebum regulation, dry skin gets deep hydration, aging skin gets peptide support, and sensitive skin benefits from the barrier-strengthening and anti-inflammatory properties of Niacinamide. It is the most versatile active serum in the range.

Niacinamide (10%)
Niacinamide (Vitamin B3)

At 10% concentration — the most studied, effective level for skincare. Minimises pore appearance, regulates sebum, brightens uneven tone by interrupting melanin transfer, reduces redness and inflammation, and strengthens the skin barrier. A true multi-tasking active.

Hyaluronic Acid
Sodium Hyaluronate

Delivered at multiple molecular weights to hydrate at different skin depths. High molecular weight provides surface plumping. Low molecular weight penetrates deeper for lasting moisture. Provides the hydration layer that balances the active nature of Niacinamide and Zinc.

Zinc PCA
Zinc PCA

Regulates sebum production at the follicle level, reducing shine and minimising pore clogging. Works synergistically with Niacinamide — together they are one of the most effective combinations for oily and acne-prone skin management.

Peptides

Signal peptides that stimulate collagen and elastin synthesis. Palmitoyl Tripeptide-1 supports skin firmness; Palmitoyl Tetrapeptide-7 reduces the signs of inflammation that accelerate skin ageing. A premium addition that differentiates this serum from basic niacinamide formulas.

How to use

Usage instructions

1
Cleanse and optionally tone the face. Apply to clean, slightly damp skin for best absorption of the Hyaluronic Acid.
2
Dispense 3–4 drops onto fingertips or the palm. Dot across the forehead, both cheeks, nose, and chin.
3
Pat gently into the skin. Avoid rubbing — patting allows the actives to absorb without disrupting the skin barrier.
4
Follow with moisturiser to seal in the serum and provide an additional hydration and protection layer. Allow the serum 60 seconds to absorb before applying moisturiser.
5
Use morning and evening. Niacinamide is not photosensitising so it can be used at any time of day. For maximum results, use consistently twice daily.
