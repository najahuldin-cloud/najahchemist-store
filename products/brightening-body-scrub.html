About this product

What is the Brightening Body Scrub?

The Najah Chemist Brightening Body Scrub combines physical and chemical exfoliation for maximum brightening effect. Sugar granules physically remove dead skin cells to reveal fresh skin underneath, while Papaya Enzyme (Papain) chemically dissolves surface skin proteins for a smoother finish. Kojic Acid inhibits melanin production to fade dark spots over time, and Turmeric provides anti-inflammatory antioxidant support. Vitamin C brightens and protects while Shea Butter and Coconut Oil replenish moisture so the skin feels soft immediately after rinsing.

The result is a 2-in-1 product: exfoliation and brightening in one shower. Customers see visibly brighter, softer skin after the first use — making this one of the easiest skincare products to get positive reviews on and repeat orders for.

⚠ Avoid broken or irritated skin. Do not use on sunburned skin. Rinse thoroughly after use. After exfoliating, apply SPF if going outdoors — freshly exfoliated skin is more sensitive to UV. Keep out of reach of children.

Key ingredients & benefits

Full INCI: Sucrose, Cocos Nucifera (Coconut) Oil, Butyrospermum Parkii (Shea) Butter, Carica Papaya (Papaya) Fruit Extract, Kojic Acid, Curcuma Longa (Turmeric) Root Extract, Ascorbic Acid (Vitamin C), Tocopherol (Vitamin E), Fragrance

Papaya Enzyme
Carica Papaya Fruit Extract (Papain)

Natural enzymatic exfoliant that dissolves dead skin proteins at the surface. Papain works gently but effectively to smooth texture and reveal brighter skin, with less friction than harsh physical scrubs. Naturally derived from papaya.

Kojic Acid
Kojic Acid

Inhibits tyrosinase — the enzyme responsible for melanin production. Regular use fades dark spots, post-inflammatory hyperpigmentation, and uneven skin tone over time. One of the most widely used skin brightening actives in professional skincare.

Turmeric
Curcuma Longa Root Extract

Anti-inflammatory antioxidant that reduces redness and skin inflammation while contributing to an overall brightening effect. Curcumin in turmeric inhibits melanin synthesis and protects skin cells from oxidative damage.

Vitamin C
Ascorbic Acid

Potent antioxidant that brightens skin tone by inhibiting melanin production and neutralising free radicals from UV exposure. Works synergistically with Kojic Acid for a combined brightening effect greater than either ingredient alone.

How to use

Usage instructions

1
Apply to damp skin in the shower. Wet skin allows the sugar to dissolve gradually and the oils to spread easily.
2
Massage in circular motions for 1–2 minutes, focusing on areas with dark spots or rough texture — knees, elbows, underarms, inner thighs.
3
Rinse thoroughly with warm water. Pat skin dry gently — do not rub, as the skin is freshly exfoliated.
4
Use 2–3 times per week for best results. Follow with a body moisturiser or body butter to seal in hydration on freshly exfoliated skin.
5
Apply SPF 30+ before sun exposure after exfoliating. Freshly exfoliated skin is more photosensitive — SPF protection prevents post-exfoliation darkening.
