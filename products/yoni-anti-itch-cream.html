About this product

What is the Yoni Anti-Itch Cream?

The Najah Chemist Yoni Anti-Itch Cream is a targeted soothing cream formulated to provide fast relief from external intimate itching and irritation. Calamine and Zinc Oxide work together to calm skin on contact, reducing the urge to itch while protecting the delicate intimate skin barrier. Allantoin promotes healing of any irritated or broken skin, while Aloe Vera delivers instant soothing hydration.

Unlike general-purpose creams, this formula is specifically designed for the sensitivity of intimate skin — using gentle, tested ingredients at concentrations appropriate for the intimate area. It provides immediate comfort while supporting the skin's recovery, making it suitable for daily use when needed.

⚠ For external use only. Do not use internally. Discontinue use if irritation worsens or does not improve. Consult a healthcare provider if symptoms persist. Keep out of reach of children.

Zinc Oxide
Zinc Oxide

A gentle mineral ingredient widely used in barrier creams and intimate skin products. Creates a protective barrier over irritated skin while reducing inflammation and the sensation of itching. Safe and effective for sensitive intimate skin.

Allantoin
Allantoin

A skin-healing ingredient derived from the comfrey plant. Promotes the healing of irritated and damaged skin cells, soothes discomfort, and supports skin regeneration. Widely used in sensitive skin formulations for its gentle yet effective healing properties.

Calamine
Calamine (Zinc Carbonate)

The classic calming ingredient used in anti-itch products. Works by cooling the skin on contact and creating a mild astringent effect that reduces itching quickly. Calamine has been trusted for intimate and sensitive skin itching relief for generations.

Aloe Vera
Aloe Barbadensis Leaf Juice

Provides instant soothing hydration and cooling relief on contact. Anti-inflammatory properties reduce redness and discomfort. A cornerstone of sensitive intimate skin care — calms irritated skin while keeping it hydrated and comfortable.

Usage instructions

How to use it

1
Wash hands thoroughly before application.
2
Apply a small amount to the external intimate area where itching or irritation is present.
3
Gently massage until the cream is absorbed into the skin.
4
Use up to 3 times daily or as needed for relief.
5
Do not use internally. If symptoms persist or worsen, consult a healthcare provider.
