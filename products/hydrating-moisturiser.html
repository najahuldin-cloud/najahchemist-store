About this product

What is the Najah Chemist Hydrating Moisturiser?

The Najah Chemist Hydrating Moisturiser is a lightweight, daily-use face cream formulated with Aloe Vera, Hyaluronic Acid, Panthenol, and Rice Protein to deliver deep hydration without clogging pores or leaving a greasy finish.

The formula also contains Allantoin and Vitamin E to soothe, protect, and nourish the skin barrier. Cucumber and Organic Herbal Extract calm irritated skin, making this moisturiser suitable for all skin types including sensitive and oily.

Manufactured in Jamaica by Najah Chemist, this moisturiser is available wholesale for private label brands, spas, salons, and skincare retailers.
