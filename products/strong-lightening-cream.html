About this product

What is the Najah Chemist Strong Lightening Cream?

The Najah Chemist Strong Lightening Cream is an advanced skin brightening cream formulated with a powerful combination of actives — Kojic Acid, Salicylic Acid, Lactic Acid, Lemon Extract, and Zinc — to target stubborn dark spots, hyperpigmentation, and uneven skin tone that standard products cannot address.

The formula combines melanin-inhibiting ingredients with mild chemical exfoliants to accelerate skin cell turnover, revealing brighter, more even skin beneath. Allantoin and Vitamin E are included to soothe and protect the skin throughout the lightening process.

Manufactured in Jamaica by Najah Chemist, this cream is available wholesale for private label brands, spas, salons, and skincare retailers.
