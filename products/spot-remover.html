About this product

What is the Najah Chemist Spot Remover?

A targeted treatment cream formulated specifically to fade dark spots and hyperpigmentation. Alpha Arbutin and Kojic Acid work together as the most effective combination of melanin inhibitors available in skincare — one from natural arbutin, one from kojic acid fermentation. Niacinamide supports brightening, reduces inflammation, and strengthens the skin barrier. Allantoin promotes skin healing. Used morning and evening with SPF during the day, visible results are typically seen in 4–6 weeks.
