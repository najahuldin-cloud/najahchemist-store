About this product

What is the Najah Chemist Beard Oil?

The Najah Chemist Beard Oil is a premium daily conditioning oil for beard hair and the skin beneath. Argan Oil — known as "liquid gold" — provides intense shine and softness to coarse beard hair while Jojoba Oil mimics the skin's natural sebum to moisturise and protect the skin underneath. Castor Oil adds thickness and nourishment to the beard hair shaft while supporting follicle health. Vitamin E protects against oxidative damage from environmental factors.

Beard itch, dry flaking skin under the beard, and coarse unmanageable hair are the three main complaints of men with beards. This formula addresses all three. Applied daily to a clean, slightly damp beard, it absorbs quickly without leaving a greasy residue — the beard looks groomed and healthy without feeling oily.

Formulation

Key ingredients & benefits

Full INCI: Argania Spinosa (Argan) Kernel Oil, Simmondsia Chinensis (Jojoba) Seed Oil, Ricinus Communis (Castor) Seed Oil, Tocopherol (Vitamin E), Essential Oil Blend

Argan Oil
Argania Spinosa Kernel Oil

The gold standard for hair shine and softness. Rich in fatty acids and Vitamin E, Argan Oil penetrates the hair shaft to soften coarse beard hair, add natural shine, and reduce frizz and roughness. Absorbs quickly without greasiness.

Jojoba Oil
Simmondsia Chinensis Seed Oil

Closest to the skin's natural sebum of any plant oil. Moisturises the skin beneath the beard, reduces beard itch and flaking, and regulates the skin's natural oil production. Non-comedogenic — won't clog pores under the beard.

Castor Oil
Ricinus Communis Seed Oil

Thick, nutrient-rich oil that coats the beard hair shaft to increase thickness and resilience. Rich in ricinoleic acid, which has anti-inflammatory properties and may support healthy follicle circulation for beard growth over time.

Vitamin E
Tocopherol

Antioxidant that protects beard hair and skin from environmental oxidative damage. Also acts as a natural preservative to extend the shelf life of the carrier oils in the formula.

How to use

Usage instructions

1
Start with a clean beard. Use after washing with a beard shampoo or cleanser. Pat the beard dry with a towel — slightly damp is ideal.
2
Dispense 3–6 drops into the palm of your hand depending on beard length. Short beard = 3 drops, long beard = 5–6 drops.
3
Rub palms together to warm the oil, then work it through the beard from roots to tips, massaging into the skin underneath.
4
Comb or brush through the beard to distribute evenly and style as desired. Use daily for best conditioning results.
