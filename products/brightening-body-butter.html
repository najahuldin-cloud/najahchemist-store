About this product

What is the Brightening Body Butter?

The Najah Chemist Brightening Body Butter combines a rich emollient base with active brightening ingredients for a dual-action daily moisturiser. Shea Butter and Cocoa Butter provide the deep, long-lasting moisture that Jamaican skin needs — especially in areas prone to dryness like elbows, knees, and hands. Kojic Acid inhibits melanin production to fade dark spots, while Turmeric and Vitamin C provide antioxidant brightening support. Niacinamide adds further brightening and skin barrier benefits.

The result is a body butter customers feel and see working simultaneously. The moisture is instant; the brightening builds over 4–8 weeks of daily use. This combination drives consistent repeat purchases — customers run out quickly and reorder because they see results.

How to use

Usage instructions

1
Apply to clean skin after bathing or showering while the skin is still slightly damp. Damp skin locks in moisture more effectively.
2
Scoop a small amount and warm between palms. Massage into skin in upward circular motions, focusing on dark spots, uneven areas, and dry zones.
3
Use daily — morning and evening for fastest results. Brightening actives accumulate with consistent use, so regularity is key.
4
Apply SPF 30+ during the day when using brightening products. Sun exposure can reverse brightening progress — SPF protects and preserves the results.
