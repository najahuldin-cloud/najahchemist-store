Who this is for

Customisation is right for you if any of these fit

  • You want your own version of an existing Najah Chemist product
  • You want to add specific actives for targeted results
  • You want a unique scent, color, or texture
  • You're building a serious long-term brand and need something different from standard private label
Customisation options

What you can customise

⚗️
Texture & Consistency

Thicker creams, lightweight lotions, gel or oil-based textures, more or less foam in cleansers. We adjust the formula so your product delivers exactly the user experience your customers expect.

🔬
Active Ingredients

Add brightening agents (Kojic Acid, Mandelic Acid, Vitamin C), acne actives (Salicylic Acid, Niacinamide), hydration boosters, or targeted treatments for stretch marks, anti-aging, and skin repair.

J$4,000 per active added
🌸
Fragrance & Scent

Choose from our in-stock fragrances at no extra charge. Exotic or specialty fragrances (vanilla, luxury blends) start from J$1,500 per gallon. Fragrance-free versions available on request.

In-stock: free  ·  Specialty: from J$1,500/gal
🎨
Color & Appearance

Natural tones or vibrant color options. Clean minimal look or bold product appeal. Color customisation is available at no extra charge when using in-stock colorants.

In-stock colorants: free
