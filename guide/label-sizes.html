Design Tips
  • Use Canva to design your labels — it's free and easy
  • Set your canvas to the exact label dimensions before designing
  • Use 300 DPI for print quality
  • Add a bleed of 0.125" around the edges
  • Include: Product name, your brand name, net weight/volume, ingredients, how to use, your contact info
  • For Jamaica: no specific regulatory label requirements for cosmetics, but best practice is to include all of the above
