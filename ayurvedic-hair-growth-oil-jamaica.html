About this product

What is the Salicylic Acid Soap?

The Najah Chemist Salicylic Acid Soap is a cold process bar formulated to target acne, breakouts, and congested skin. Salicylic Acid is a beta hydroxy acid (BHA) that penetrates inside the pore to dissolve excess oil and dead skin cells — the root cause of blackheads, whiteheads, and acne breakouts. Zinc regulates sebum production while Cucumber Extract soothes redness and inflammation.

Acne is one of the most common skincare concerns among young Jamaicans aged 15–35 — making this soap a fast-selling product for brands targeting this demographic. Customers who see results within the first two weeks become loyal repeat buyers.

⚠ External use only. Avoid contact with eyes. Not for use on broken, cracked, or severely inflamed skin. Limit use to once daily for sensitive skin — overuse may cause dryness. Discontinue if irritation occurs. Recommend a moisturiser after use. Sunscreen recommended during the day. Keep out of reach of children. Store in a cool dry place.

About this product

What is the Ayurvedic Hair Growth Oil?

The Najah Chemist Ayurvedic Hair Growth Oil is a nourishing scalp and hair treatment oil inspired by Ayurvedic hair care traditions. Ricinus Communis (Castor) Oil is the hero ingredient — one of the most widely trusted oils for hair growth, thickness, and scalp health. Combined with Cocos Nucifera (Coconut) Oil for penetrating nourishment and Helianthus Annuus (Sunflower) Oil as a lightweight carrier, this oil delivers deep conditioning without excessive heaviness.

Herbal Extracts and Essential Oils add targeted scalp support — stimulating circulation, reducing scalp irritation, and enhancing the hair care experience with a therapeutic scent. This oil positions strongly in both the natural hair care and wellness product segments — two rapidly growing markets in Jamaica and the wider Caribbean.

⚠ For external scalp and hair use only. Do not apply to the face, eye area, or mucous membranes. Patch test on a small area of scalp before first use — especially for those with sensitive skin or known essential oil sensitivities. Avoid contact with eyes — rinse immediately with water if contact occurs. Do not use on broken or irritated scalp without medical guidance. Discontinue if irritation, itching, or redness occurs. Keep out of reach of children. Some Essential Oils may not be suitable for use during pregnancy — advise customers to consult a healthcare provider if pregnant.

Usage instructions

How to use it

1
Patch test first — apply a small amount to the scalp or inner wrist 24 hours before first full use. Check for any reaction.
2
Apply to scalp — use fingertips or a dropper to apply directly to the scalp in sections. Do not apply to the eye area.
3
Massage gently — massage into the scalp in circular motions for 3–5 minutes to stimulate circulation and enhance absorption.
4
Apply to hair lengths — work any remaining oil through the hair ends to condition and reduce breakage.
5
Use 2–3 times per week — leave on overnight for intensive treatment or rinse after 30 minutes. Wash out with shampoo as part of a regular hair care routine.
