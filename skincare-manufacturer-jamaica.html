Common questions

Answered directly

What is a private label skincare manufacturer?
A private label skincare manufacturer produces formulas in bulk that clients repackage and sell under their own brand name. Najah Chemist is a private label skincare manufacturer in Jamaica — we produce the formula, you own the brand, the label, and the customer relationship. Your name goes on the product. Our formula goes inside it.
What products does Najah Chemist manufacture?
We manufacture across five categories: brightening (turmeric & kojic soap, skin lightening cream, dark spot remover, niacinamide cleanser), acne treatment (glycolic acid toner, serums, cleanser), hydrating skincare (hyaluronic acid serum, moisturiser, toners), feminine care (yoni wash, yoni oil, steam herbs, boric acid capsules), and body care (body butter, scrub, body oil). We also manufacture men's grooming products including beard oil, beard balm, and 3-in-1 body wash.
Do I need experience to work with a skincare manufacturer in Jamaica?
No. Najah Chemist is designed specifically for beginners. We handle all formulation and production. You choose your product from our ready-made catalogue, we manufacture it in bulk, and you repackage it under your own brand name. No lab, no chemistry background, and no large investment required to get started.
How long does manufacturing take?
Most orders are ready within 5–10 business days after payment and product selection. We ship island-wide via Knutsford Express and Zipmail. Kingston and St. Andrew orders can be arranged for hand delivery. We do not currently ship internationally.
How is this different from buying wholesale products?
When you buy wholesale, you resell someone else's branded product. With private label manufacturing at Najah Chemist, we produce the formula and you sell it under your own brand name. You set the price, you own the customer relationship, and you build equity in your own brand — not someone else's.
