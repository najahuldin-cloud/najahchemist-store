Key actives in this formula

What makes this soap work

This is a professional-grade formulation — not a handmade or all-natural soap. It uses clinically recognised brightening actives.

⚗️
Kojic Acid
The primary brightening active. Kojic acid is a well-established ingredient that inhibits the enzyme tyrosinase, reducing melanin production. Consistent use helps fade dark spots, post-acne marks, and hyperpigmentation across the body and face. It is one of the most studied brightening actives in professional skincare formulation.
🌾
Turmeric
Anti-inflammatory support. Turmeric contributes curcumin, which provides secondary brightening support and helps calm skin inflammation. In combination with kojic acid, it addresses two pathways of hyperpigmentation — melanin production and the inflammation that triggers it. The result is a more comprehensive brightening effect than either active alone.
Formulation note: This soap is produced by Najah Chemist using carefully selected ingredients including actives like kojic acid. It is a professional-grade formula — not a homemade product, not all-natural, and not suited for brands positioning as "organic." It is designed for brands focused on results-driven brightening.

Common questions

Answered directly

What actives are in the Turmeric & Kojic Acid Soap?
The formulation uses kojic acid as the primary brightening active and turmeric as a supporting anti-inflammatory ingredient. Kojic acid inhibits melanin production to fade dark spots and hyperpigmentation. Turmeric adds secondary brightening and anti-inflammatory properties. This is a professional-grade formula — not a handmade or all-natural product. It contains carefully selected actives designed for consistent, measurable brightening results.
How is the soap sold? What is the minimum order?
The Turmeric & Kojic Acid Soap is sold in sets of 10 × 4oz bars. Each bar is individually wrapped and ready to resell. The minimum order is one set of 10 bars. There is no minimum beyond one set — which makes it accessible for new brands testing the product before scaling their orders.
Can I sell this under my own brand name?
Yes. This product is available for private label. You receive the bars individually wrapped. You source your own labels and apply them before selling. The formula is made by Najah Chemist — you sell it under your own brand name. Najah Chemist does not appear on the product unless you choose to include our name as the manufacturer.
Is this soap suitable for the face?
This soap can be used on the body and face, but as with any active-ingredient product, individual sensitivity varies. Kojic acid can cause irritation in people with sensitive skin when used on the face at higher concentrations. We recommend advising your customers to patch test before regular facial use. Selling guidelines should be based on your own assessment of your customer base.
How long does manufacturing and delivery take?
Most orders are ready within 5–10 business days after payment is confirmed. We ship island-wide across Jamaica via Knutsford Express and Zipmail. Kingston and St. Andrew orders can be arranged for hand delivery. We do not currently ship internationally.
What is the difference between this soap and the Skin Lightening Soap?
Both are brightening bar soaps. The Turmeric & Kojic Acid Soap combines kojic acid with turmeric, targeting hyperpigmentation and skin inflammation. The Skin Lightening Soap has a different active ingredient profile focused on skin tone evenness. Many brands stock both to offer customers a choice.
