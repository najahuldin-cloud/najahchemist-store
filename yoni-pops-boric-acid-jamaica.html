About this product

What are Yoni Pops?

Yoni Pops are boric acid suppositories in a vegetable capsule, formulated with Aloe Extract, Herbal Extracts, and Mineral Salts. Boric acid suppositories are widely used to support vaginal pH balance — particularly popular with women who experience recurring pH disruption or want a natural wellness routine.

Yoni Pops are one of the most searched feminine care products in Jamaica and across the Caribbean. Brand owners who carry this product consistently report strong sales and high repeat rates — customers who find a product that works come back monthly.

⚠ Critical safety information — read before selling this product.
  • For vaginal use only — do NOT take orally. Boric acid is toxic if swallowed.
  • Keep out of reach of children — Boric Acid is harmful and potentially fatal if ingested by children
  • Do NOT use during pregnancy — contraindicated for pregnant women
  • Not for use on broken, irritated, or infected tissue
  • Consult a healthcare provider before use — especially for recurring infections
  • Not a substitute for medical diagnosis or treatment
  • Discontinue if irritation, burning, or unusual symptoms occur
  • Store out of reach of children in a cool, dry place

Usage instructions

How to use it

Include these instructions on your product label in full. This product requires clear, complete directions for safe use.

1
Wash hands thoroughly — always wash hands before and after handling.
2
Insert vaginally — insert one capsule into the vagina as far as comfortably possible, preferably at bedtime.
3
Allow to dissolve — the vegetable capsule dissolves naturally with body moisture. Wear a pantyliner as discharge may occur.
4
Use as directed — typically used for 7–14 consecutive nights or as advised by a healthcare provider. Do not exceed recommended duration without medical guidance.
