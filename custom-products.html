The Process

How It Works

Four straightforward steps from your idea to your finished product.

1
Consultation

We discuss your brand, target market, and product vision via WhatsApp or phone. No lab knowledge required — just tell us what you want to create.

2
Formula Development

We develop a custom formula using our professional ingredient stock. Your formula is proprietary — it belongs to your brand.

3
Sample & Review

You receive a 2oz sample to test. Up to 2 adjustment rounds are included in the R&D fee so you can fine-tune the product before going to bulk.

4
Bulk Production

Once you approve the formula, place your bulk order. Minimum first order is 5 gallons. We manufacture, you brand and sell.
