# POST_DEPLOY_WEEK1_PLAYBOOK.md — First 7 Days

How to use Jarvis the week after deploy. Pair with `JARVIS_WEEK1_FEEDBACK.md` (log daily)
and `PHASE4_WEEK1_SUCCESS.md` (targets).

## Day 1
- **Check first:** 🔥 **Founder Focus** (the one action) → do it. Then ⏱ **Next 15 Minutes** (top 3).
- **Then glance:** 🎯 Revenue Target (gap + progress) and ⚠ Revenue at Risk (worst offender).
- **Ignore today:** the Lead Command Center long table, Revenue-by-Offer breakdown, and any Cold leads — don't get pulled into analysis. No re-tuning, no "fixing" the dashboard.
- **Counts as success:** you did the Founder Focus action and at least one Next-15 action, in <15 minutes total.

## Days 2–3 — Recommendation tracking
- Each morning: do Founder Focus + log it in the feedback file (did it / result / correct 1–10).
- Track outcomes of Day-1 actions (reply? quote? sale? silence?).
- Note any recommendation that felt wrong and WHY (missing info? bad ranking? already-contacted?).

## Days 4–5 — Recommendation quality review
- Compare what Jarvis recommended vs what actually converted.
- Are the Ready/Hot leads real opportunities, or noise? Is "Money Missed: no quote sent" (the dominant cause) actionable?
- Spot-check: any test or duplicate lead slipped into a card? (Should be zero.)

## Days 6–7 — Pattern identification
- Which recommendation TYPE paid off most (Manufacturing quote vs follow-up vs reorder)?
- Which information was missing most often?
- Did the Honest Pipeline number stay believable and useful?
